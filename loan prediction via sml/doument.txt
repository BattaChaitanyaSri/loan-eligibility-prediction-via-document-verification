		User Input + Documents
				↓
		LlamaIndex (retrieve evidence)
				↓
		PROMPT:
		  - Bank Reasoning Framework
		  - Applicant facts
		  - Document evidence
				↓
		Phi LLM (reasoning)
				↓
		Eligibility + Explanation
